BMW M5 CS – The Ultimate Luxury Sports Sedan

The BMW M5 CS is the pinnacle of the M5 lineup, a limited-edition beast that combines extreme performance with refined luxury. Packing a 4.4-liter twin-turbo V8 producing 635 hp and 750 Nm (470 lb-ft) of torque, it rockets from 0 to 100 km/h (62 mph) in just 3 seconds. With a top speed electronically limited to 305 km/h (189 mph) and a weight of approximately 1,900 kg — around 70 kg lighter than the M5 Competition — the M5 CS is a masterclass in speed, agility, and precision.

Performance & Drivetrain:

  • Engine: 4.4-liter twin-turbo V8
  • Power: 635 hp (467 kW)
  • Torque: 750 Nm (553 lb-ft)
  • Transmission: 8-speed M Steptronic automatic transmission
  • Drivetrain: BMW xDrive all-wheel-drive system
  • 0-100 km/h (0-62 mph): 3.0 seconds

    • Exclusivity & Price:

    With its limited production, the M5 CS is as rare as it is powerful, commanding a price around €200,000, depending on options and configuration. Every detail, from the aggressive exterior to the luxurious interior, reflects BMW M’s commitment to performance and exclusivity.

    conclusion:

    The BMW M5 CS is more than a sedan — it’s a high-performance masterpiece, blending blistering speed, precision engineering, and unmistakable M design. For enthusiasts seeking extreme performance without compromising luxury, the M5 CS sets the standard.
