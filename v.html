Bugatti Veyron

The Bugatti Veyron, launched in 2005, redefined the automotive world by becoming the fastest production car of its time. Built as a technological showcase, it merges luxury and extreme performance.

Performance & Drivetrain

• 8.0-liter quad-turbocharged W16 engine producing 1,001 hp (Super Sport: 1,200 hp).
• 7-speed dual-clutch transmission (DSG).
• All-wheel drive for maximum traction.
• 0–100 km/h in 2.5 seconds, top speed up to 431 km/h (Super Sport).

Design & Features

• Aerodynamic body with active rear wing for high-speed stability.
• Luxurious leather interior with precision craftsmanship.
• High-tech cooling system to handle extreme heat from the engine.

Exclusivity & Price

• Limited production: Only 450 units made.
• Price when new: around €1.5 million.
• Now considered a collector's gem with rising market value.

Bottom Line

The Bugatti Veyron is more than a car—it's a milestone in automotive history, blending art, engineering, and unmatched speed.
