Mercedes-AMG GT Black Series – The Ultimate Track-Focused Supercar

The Mercedes-AMG GT Black Series is the most extreme version of the AMG GT lineup, designed to deliver uncompromising performance both on the road and the track. Powered by a hand-built 4.0-liter twin-turbo V8 producing 730 hp and 800 Nm (590 lb-ft) of torque, it accelerates from 0 to 100 km/h (62 mph) in just 3.2 seconds, with a top speed of around 325 km/h (202 mph). Its aggressive aerodynamics, lightweight construction, and track-optimized chassis make it one of the most capable and exhilarating supercars on the planet.

Performance & Drivetrain:

  • Engine: 4.0-liter twin-turbo V8
  • Power Output: 730 hp
  • Torque: 800 Nm (590 lb-ft)
  • 0-100 km/h: 3.2 seconds
  • Top Speed: Approximately 325 km/h (202 mph)
  • Transmission: 7-speed dual-clutch automatic
  • Weight: Approximately 1,550 kg (3,417 lbs)
  • Rear-wheel drive with an electronically controlled limited-slip differential for optimal traction.

Design & Features

  • Wide-body stance with functional air vents and aggressive styling cues.
  • Lightweight interior with AMG bucket seats, Alcantara steering wheel, and racing-inspired instrumentation.
  • Extensive use of carbon fiber for hood, fenders, and aerodynamic components.

Exclusivity & Price

  • Limited production model, making it highly sought-after by collectors.
  • Price starts around €335,000, reflecting its extreme performance and exclusivity.

Conclusion

The Mercedes-AMG GT Black Series is a track-honed supercar that pushes the boundaries of speed, handling, and driver engagement. For enthusiasts seeking an unmatched combination of raw power and cutting-edge engineering, it stands as the ultimate expression of Mercedes-AMG performance.
